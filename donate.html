Your Donation Makes a Difference

Every contribution to IRGCP helps us protect reticulated giraffes in Isiolo County. Your support enables us to:

Protect giraffe habitats from encroachment and degradation
Engage local communities in conservation efforts
Conduct vital research on giraffe populations and behavior
Provide education programs for schools and communities

Our Impact

Thanks to donor support, we've achieved significant milestones in giraffe conservation:

  • 15% increase in giraffe populations in the last 3 years
  • 500+ community members trained in conservation practices
  • 2,000+ acres of habitat protected and restored
  • 50+ anti-poaching patrols conducted monthly
