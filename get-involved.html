Ways to Get Involved

There are many ways you can support the Isiolo Reticulated Giraffe Conservation Program. Whether through financial contributions, volunteering your time, or partnering with us, every action makes a difference in protecting these majestic animals.

Donate

Financial Support

Your donation directly supports giraffe conservation efforts in Isiolo County. Funds are used for:

  • Habitat protection and restoration
  • Anti-poaching patrols and equipment
  • Community education programs
  • Scientific research and monitoring
Donate Now

Volunteer

Give Your Time

Join our on-the-ground conservation efforts in Isiolo. Volunteer opportunities include:

  • Field research assistance
  • Community outreach programs
  • Conservation education
  • Visitor center operations

Internships

Gain Experience

We offer internship programs for students and recent graduates in conservation-related fields. Internships provide:

  • Hands-on field experience
  • Mentorship from conservation experts
  • Research project opportunities
  • Networking in conservation circles

Partnerships

Collaborate With Us

We collaborate with organizations, businesses, and institutions to expand our conservation impact. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Corporate sponsorship programs
  • Research collaborations
  • Conservation technology partnerships
  • Educational institution partnerships
