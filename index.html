About IRGCP

Our Mission

The Isiolo Reticulated Giraffe Conservation Program (IRGCP) is dedicated to the conservation and recovery of the endangered reticulated giraffe in Isiolo County, Kenya. Inspired by initiatives like the Somali Giraffe Project and Reticulated Giraffe Awareness Program, we focus on community-based conservation through research, education, and local involvement.

Our vision is to secure a sustainable future for reticulated giraffes and their habitats through participatory conservation, research, and community engagements. Our mission is to support the protection and conservation of these magnificent animals by addressing threats such as poaching, habitat loss, and human-wildlife conflict.
